what does your #anxiety feel like to you?

  • @carrot: It is a group of invisible monsters pulling you back saying, "Wait! That's scary!" Keeping you from being the person you want to be.
  • @broccoli: To be anxious is to be dragged down and boxed in by irrational fear. And when fear is on your mind, you can think of nothing else. It takes over your mind, body, and world.
  • @lettuce: It is as if there are a dozen other programs running on your computer at once. It distracts you and slows you down. It is a constant static of negative thoughts running through the back of your mind.
  • @tomato: It is voices constantly telling you all the ways in which something can go wrong.
